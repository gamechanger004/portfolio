Fresh detect

As part of my journey in the field of computer vision and IoT, I initiated and led the development of Fresh Detect, a system designed to monitor and predict the shelf life of fruits and vegetables. sign language detection

The Sign Language Detection project is a computer vision application designed to recognize and interpret sign language gestures, enabling communication between individuals with hearing impairments and those who may not understand traditional sign language. This project aims to bridge communication gaps and promote inclusivity through technology.
